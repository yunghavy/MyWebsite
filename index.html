About

Jaffer Kimutai is an internationally acclaimed photographer, filmmaker, YouTube creator and entrepreneur based in Nairobi, Kenya. Jaffer uses his platforms to inspire and help people pursue their passions behind the lens.

Photographer & Web Developer.

Talk about Jack of all trades, Jaffer is a member of all industries i.e technology, business, art and the financial world.

Jaffer's work can be found in a number of outlets but a notable personal achievement for this Kenyan was having his work featured on Youpic and 500px at a photographers project that was global.

He is also the founder of Sicc Frequencies, a platform for musicians and story tellers. He is also the founder of Havierwrld Entertainment, a production company that produces short films and other film related productions

Services

Some of the services I offer. I serve you with best quality work.

Photography

As a photographer, I strive to capture moments that convey emotions, tell stories, and leave a lasting impression on viewers. I continuously seek inspiration, explore new techniques, and experiment with different styles to evolve as an artist and deliver captivating visual experiences through my photographs.

Cinematography

My role as a cinematographer involves collaborating closely with directors, production designers, and other members of the filmmaking team to bring the director's vision to life. I carefully consider the mood, tone, and narrative of a film or video project to make creative decisions that enhance the storytelling and evoke desired emotions.

Color grading & Editing

By combining color grading and editing techniques, I can transform raw media into visually stunning and impactful final products, whether it's a film, video, or photographic project. I strive to enhance the storytelling, evoke emotions, and create a cohesive visual experience for the audience.

Graphic Designing

With a keen eye for aesthetics and a deep understanding of design principles, I have the ability to translate ideas into compelling visuals that resonate with the target audience. I utilize design software such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign to create and manipulate graphics, illustrations, and layouts.

Software Development

I possess a strong technical skill set, problem-solving abilities, and a commitment to creating reliable and efficient software applications. I am dedicated to delivering high-quality solutions that meet client expectations and contribute to the advancement of technology.

Script Writer

Whether it's a captivating screenplay, a memorable commercial, or a compelling video narrative, my expertise in scriptwriting allows me to create content that grabs attention, communicates ideas clearly, and leaves a lasting impression. With a passion for storytelling and a dedication to excellence, I bring stories to life through the power of the written word.
