About

Jaffer Kimutai is an internationally acclaimed photographer, filmmaker, YouTube creator and entrepreneur based in Nairobi, Kenya. Jaffer uses his platforms to inspire and help people pursue their passions behind the lens.

Photographer & Web Developer.

Talk about Jack of all trades, Jaffer a.k.a "Yung Havy" is a member of all industries i.e technology, business, art and the financial world.

Jaffer's work can be found in a number of outlets but a notable personal achievement for this Kenyan was having his work featured on Youpic and 500px at a photographers project that was global.

He is also the founder of Sicc Frequencies, a platform for musicians and story tellers. He is also the founder of Havierwrld Entertainment, a production company that produces short films and other film related productions. Yung Havy is a brand that he discovered in 2018 as he started his photography hobby.

Services

Some of the services I offer. I serve you with best quality work.

Photography

I specialize in portrait photography, event photography, and product photography.

I have experience with a variety of lighting setups, including natural light, studio lighting, and location lighting.

I use Adobe Photoshop and Lightroom to edit my photographs.

Cinematography

I use a variety of camera equipment, including DSLRs, mirrorless cameras, and cinema cameras.

I also have a wide range of lenses, from wide-angle lenses to telephoto lenses to prime lenses.

I believe that cinematography is a powerful tool that can be used to create a specific mood or atmosphere.

Color grading & Editing

I use DaVinci Resolve for color grading and Adobe Premiere Pro for editing.

I have experience with a variety of color grading and editing techniques.

I start by understanding the client's vision for their project, then use color grading and editing to bring that vision to life.

Graphic Designing

I am proficient in a variety of design software, including Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and Sketch.

I use these software to create a wide range of graphics, from logos and branding to marketing materials and website designs.

I am a creative thinker with a strong problem-solving skills.I am able to take a client's vision and translate it into a compelling visual representation.

Software Development
